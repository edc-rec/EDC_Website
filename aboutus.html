Footprints in Time

EDC's Commanding Entrepreneurial Narrative

Founding Years

2015 - 2016

The seeds of entrepreneurship were sown in 2015 as Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) envisioned a platform to nurture entrepreneurial spirit among students.

EDC was established with a vision to empower students, create job opportunities, and contribute to the nation's development.

Initial activities included meetings with faculty, agenda discussions, and the launch of E-Awareness campaigns.

E-conclave'16

March 10th and 11th, 2016 witnessed one of the greatest events, E-Conclave'16 that inspired and exhilarated the idea of entrepreneurship.

Our event featured a wide range of activities, including guest lectures, panel discussions, motivational talks, workshops, debates and engaging showcases of start-ups.

With an impressive turnout of approximately 500 participants and the participation of 15 innovative start-ups, our event was a resounding success.

This achievement is a testament to the collaborative efforts of our dedicated team, with unwavering support from the management, effective coordination by the E-Cell, and the remarkable dedication of our students.

We are proud to announce that our event has earned national recognition as a prestigious E-Conclave hosted at REC.

E- Week 2016

"E Week '16" took place over four dynamic days in April 2016, offering an array of engaging activities, including workshops, panel discussions, and seminars.

The event had a clear focus on promoting entrepreneurship, innovation, and collaboration, aiming to inspire and educate participants in these areas.

"E Week '16" attracted a diverse audience, including students, entrepreneurs, and professionals, creating a platform for meaningful knowledge exchange and networking opportunities.

The event left a lasting impact by fostering a spirit of creativity and entrepreneurial enthusiasm, contributing to the growth and development of the entrepreneurial community it targeted.

E-WEEK'18

Day 1: Motivational speaker Jaikanth M.C. shared his success story and valuable insights, kicking off E-Week on an inspiring note.

Day 2: The day revolved around expanding the mind through new experiences, featuring entrepreneurial games and a business quiz.

Day 3: Participants engaged in a business quiz, highlighting the importance of knowledge in the business world.

Day 4: The focus shifted to risk management, with various games testing decision-making abilities under pressure.

Day 5: Similar to "Shark Tank," participants pitched their business ideas to a judging panel, making it the ultimate test of entrepreneurship skills.

E-WEEK'19

Day 1 : E-Awareness Camp'19

- On the first day, Mr. Lokeshwaran S., Founder of Receptacle Enterprises Private Limited, served as the speaker, aiming to motivate first-year students towards entrepreneurship.

Day 2 : Adzap

- The second day introduced the Adzap challenge, where participants had to advertise a product within three minutes. The emphasis was on creativity, productivity, and managerial skills.

Day 3 : Dexter Tester

- Day three featured the Dexter Tester 3.0 event, designed to test participants' risk-taking abilities.

Day 4 : H.U.S.T.L.E

- The focus shifted to emulating the Start-Up ecosystem with the H.U.S.T.L.E Business Plan Competition. The outcome was an informative competition refining business ideas.

Day 5 : Business Quiz

- The fifth day involved a Business Quiz that evaluated participants' knowledge and interest in entrepreneurship, similar to the format of a quiz show.

E-CONCLAVE’19

Day 1 : Ideas Rule:

Inauguration by Dr. V. Murali Baskar, featuring Chief Guest Mr. Ratheesh Krishnan, commenced E-CONCLAVE’19, with a focus on entrepreneurship through workshops, guest lectures, and stand-up comedy sessions.

Day 2 : Make It Happen:

E-CONCLAVE’19 shifted its focus to actionable steps on the second day, with a Start-Up and Idea Showcase, along with workshops delving into Design Thinking and Business Plans.

Day 3 : Zero to Hero:

The concluding day brought entrepreneurs sharing success stories, covering diverse genres from grassroots initiatives to social entrepreneurship, providing a holistic view of the entrepreneurial landscape.
