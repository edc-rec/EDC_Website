Footprints in Time

EDC's Commanding Entrepreneurial Narrative

Founding Years

The seeds of entrepreneurship were sown in 2015 as Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) envisioned a platform to nurture entrepreneurial spirit among students.

EDC was established with a vision to empower students, create job opportunities, and contribute to the nation's development.

Initial activities included meetings with faculty, agenda discussions, and the launch of E-Awareness campaigns.

Growth and Expansion

EDC quickly gained momentum, organizing events like E-Conclave, E-Awareness campaigns, and E-Week, creating a buzz around entrepreneurship on the campus.

The launch of the EDC Course marked a pivotal moment, providing structured education on entrepreneurship to REC students.

The board members actively engaged in faculty meetings, and an E-Cell Membership drive further solidified the EDC's presence.

Venture and Innovation

The years 2018-2019 witnessed the launch of VENTURE'18, a Business Hackathon, showcasing REC's commitment to fostering innovation.

E-Summit in 2020, hosted in collaboration with IIT Madras, demonstrated the college's commitment to national-level engagement in entrepreneurship.

Adapting to Challenges

Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, EDC continued its mission through online events like EQ-TRIVIA'20 and Eclub'sfter C'19.

The Entrepreneurial Opportunities After COVID-19 initiative reflected the cell's adaptability and resilience.

Expanding Horizons

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell entered the academic year 2022-2023 with a renewed focus on impactful initiatives.

The recruitment drive, national-level symposium E STAR 2022, and events like PITCH DECK EVENT at IIT Bombay NEC showcased REC's commitment to expanding the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Notable incubation services were offered to promising startups like Ingènuir's Café, Student's Corner, and Magnum Technologies.
